Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2025

Overview

Lift is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Workout Data

We store your workout information locally on your device, including:

  • Exercise names, sets, reps, and weights
  • Workout dates and duration
  • Rest periods and RIR (Reps in Reserve)
  • Custom notes and tags

Health Data

With your permission, Lift accesses:

  • Body weight data from Apple Health
  • Workout data for integration purposes

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Track and display your workout progress
  • Calculate analytics like volume, 1RM estimations, and trends
  • Provide personalized insights and recommendations
  • Sync data across your devices (if iCloud is enabled)

Data Storage and Security

All workout data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled. We do not have access to your workout data, and we do not store any personal information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Lift does not share your data with any third-party services or advertisers. Your workout data remains completely private to you.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Export all your workout data at any time
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Disable Apple Health integration
  • Turn off iCloud sync
