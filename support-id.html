About Support ID

Support ID is a code your device creates when you choose to share App Analytics. It lets Support locate the diagnostic logs your app has already sent, so issues you report can be investigated faster and more accurately.

  • Support ID connects your support request to existing, anonymous diagnostics.
  • Support ID does not identify you personally.

Requirements

To use Support ID, you need:

  • The app installed and able to access its Settings.
  • Share App Analytics turned on in your app settings.

Note: If you do not share App Analytics, a Support ID will not be created.

Find and Share Your Support ID

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to Account.
  3. Tap Data & Storage.
  4. Find Support ID.
  5. When you contact Support, provide the Support ID exactly as shown.

Tip: Tab the Support ID to copy it and avoid transcription errors.

What Support ID Is Not

  • It does not contain your name, email, Apple ID, or any personal details.
  • It cannot be used to track you across different apps or services.
  • It changes if you delete and reinstall the app. Past data cannot be linked to new installations.

How Support ID Is Created

To keep Support ID stable for troubleshooting and private for your identity, the app uses a multi-step process:

  1. Start with a system-provided, app-scoped device identifier.
  2. Generate a random, app-specific salt (a secret value used once), stored only on your device.
  3. Combine the identifier and salt, then process them with a strong cryptographic hash function (a one-way algorithm).
  4. Shorten the result into the Support ID shown in the app's Settings.

This design ensures:

  • The Support ID looks different from the underlying system identifier.
  • The process cannot be reversed to reveal your device or personal information.
  • Reinstalling the app creates a new salt and a new Support ID, preventing links to prior installations.

Privacy by Design

By design, your Support ID is:

  • Anonymous: it cannot be traced back to you personally.
  • Non-persistent across reinstalls: each installation creates a new Support ID.
  • Purpose-limited: used only to match your request to diagnostic logs for troubleshooting.

These protections are repeated here for clarity: Support ID is anonymous, it resets on reinstall, and it is used only for support.

Reinstalling the App

  • If you delete and reinstall the app, your Support ID changes.
  • After reinstall, provide the new Support ID when you contact Support.
  • Previous Support IDs cannot be connected to the new installation.

Troubleshooting

If you can't find Support ID in the app:

  • Update the app to the latest version.
  • Check Account > Data & Storage.
  • Contact Support and describe where you looked.

If Support can't locate your logs with the ID you provided:

  • Confirm that Share App Analytics is turned on.
  • Verify you're reading the Support ID from the same device and app installation where the issue occurred.

Turn on Share App Analytics:

  • On your device, go to Account > Data & Storage > Analytics.
  • Turn on Share App Analytics.
  • If prompted, restart the app (close it completely and reopen it).
  • Return to Account > Data & Storage > Analytics to view your Support ID.

After reinstall:

  • Expect a new Support ID.
  • If you had an existing support case, share your new Support ID.
